-----------------fastdfs相关命令-----------------

--------启动fastdfs
    cd .../FastDFS                                      ---切到dfs根目录
    storage/fdfs_storaged conf/storage.conf             ---启动一个存储
    storage/fdfs_storaged conf/storage2.conf            ---启动另一存储
    tracker/fdfs_trackerd conf/tracker.conf             ---启动一跟踪管理进程
